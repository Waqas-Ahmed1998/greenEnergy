Green Energy produces wood pellets from both processed and unprocessed wood residues. Our processed wood raw materials include chips, bark, and sawdust by-products from wood processing facilities. Unprocessed residues include tree tops, branches, stumps, and other forestry debris remaining after the primary biomass (or the tree trunk) has been processed and shipped from the forest. These unprocessed residues would most likely otherwise go unused as a resource.
